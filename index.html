My Favorite Hobby

Playing games


My favorite hobby is playing games in my free time. I think people should play games because it encourages the mind to take a break. It also helps the mind create pathways which lead to the skills of problem solving. But the reason why I love playing games, is because it just lets me have fun. I can choose what game suits me for the day, and play that game for the time being. Games help us be rested, content, and ready for work again.
